The Scouting Effect

Scouts Australia partnered with Resilient Youth Australia to conduct the Scouts Australia Resilience Survey to investigate the impact of Scouting on the resilience of its youth members.

Through this survey, we have found that Scouts have an overall better life satisfaction than their peers, and the longer they stay in Scouts, the more resilient they are likely to become.
