Big Welcome to Glenn!

Tonight was a special night for Bundoora Scouts – we officially invested Glenn into our leadership team!

We’re thrilled to be welcoming our second new leader in just three days, after celebrating Siobhan joining us on Monday! What a fantastic week for our group.

This milestone was shared with some incredible Scouting legends: Edwin (Banyule District Commissioner), David (Banyule District Scout Leader), Bruce (our brand-new Bundoora Group Leader), and of course Hamish, the powerhouse behind our Scout section!

We’re thrilled to have Glenn on board and can’t wait to see the amazing things he’ll bring to our troop.

Welcome to the adventure, Glenn!
