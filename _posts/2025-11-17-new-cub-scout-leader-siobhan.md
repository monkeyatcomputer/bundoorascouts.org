Welcome Siobhan to the Cub Leadership Team!

We’re thrilled to announce that **Siobhan** has officially joined the Bundoora Scout Group as part of our Cub leadership team!

Siobhan brings an incredible level of enthusiasm and a true passion for helping our Cubs learn, grow, and have fun on their Scouting journey. Whether it's mastering new skills or exploring the great outdoors, we know our Cubs are in great hands.

Tonight, Siobhan was invested into the group in a special ceremony, shared with some very distinguished guests:
- **Edwin** (Banyule District Commissioner)
- **David** (Banyule District Cub Leader)
- **Bruce** (our Group Leader)
- **Kate** (our wonderful Cub Scout Leader)

It was a proud moment for Bundoora Scout Group, marking the start of a fantastic week of new leadership for our group.

Please join us in giving Siobhan a very warm welcome!
