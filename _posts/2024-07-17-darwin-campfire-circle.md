We are excited to share that the Darwin Campfire Circle at the Bundoora Scout Centre is coming to life. This project has been made possible thanks to the generous support of Eden Building & Landscaping and the Watsonia RSL.

While the progress photos might make it look easy, the construction was a true test of endurance. The team worked through some of the coldest Melbourne mornings in recent memory, battling the notoriously sticky clay mud that only Bundoora can serve up.

We are incredibly grateful for the hard work and dedication of everyone involved. We cannot wait to gather around this new campfire circle soon and share many memorable evenings together.
