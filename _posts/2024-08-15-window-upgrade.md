We are pleased to announce the completion of a significant facility upgrade at the Bundoora Scout Centre. After a brief delay while awaiting specialized glazing, our new windows have now been fully installed.

This project is a great step forward in making our hall more sustainable and secure. The new windows provide improved safety, better insulation, and increased energy efficiency, ensuring a more comfortable environment for all our members.

We extend our sincere thanks to **A40 Construction & Maintenance Group** for their dedication and hard work in completing this project.
